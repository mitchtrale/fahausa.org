Finlandia Foundation: National Sauna Week

February 15-21, 2026

The week is full of on-line events as well as some deals at public saunas - AND a chance to win a trip to Finland! With events ranging from virtual webinars and educational programs to in-person gatherings hosted by Finlandia Foundation chapters, Sauna Week celebrates sauna as a space for personal and collective renewal. At its core, National Sauna Week embodies the belief that sauna is a metaphor for a healthy society—where differences are left at the door, and all are equal in the shared embrace of warmth and community.

FAHA’s Board of Directors usually meets at 9 a.m. on the 3rd Sunday of the month (except July and December).
